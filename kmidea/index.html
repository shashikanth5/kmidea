About K Media Enterprise

K Media Enterprise, founded by Manikanta and Tharun Kaja in the USA, is a pioneering Ad-Tech company specializing in Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) advertising. We are dedicated to delivering high-impact, interactive advertising solutions through our state-of-the-art software and customized hardware. Our unique approach allows us to serve businesses of all sizes, offering unparalleled geographical reach in high-traffic locations such as restaurants, hospitals, grocery stores, and residential areas. With our expertise in digital media and wireless technology, we provide seamless, instant content updates and comprehensive display and kiosk solutions, helping brands connect with their audiences like never before.
