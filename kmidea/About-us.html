Overview

Who we are

K Media Enterprise, co-founded by Tharun Kaja and Manikanta Kaja in the USA, is an innovative Ad-Tech company revolutionizing Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) advertising. We specialize in delivering interactive, result-driven advertising solutions through our cutting-edge software and customized hardware.
Our unique selling proposition lies in our seamless client-device interaction, enabling instant content updates via an intuitive interface. We cater to all kinds of advertising budgets, ensuring maximum geographical reach for businesses, from newly established ventures to well-known brands. Our strategic ad placements in high-dwell areas such as restaurants, residential complexes, hospitals, and grocery stores ensure that your ads are seen where people are most likely to engage, offering a more impactful experience compared to intrusive online ads.
In addition to DOOH advertising, we also provide comprehensive signage and display solutions, including self-service menu boards and interactive displays, to help businesses enhance customer engagement and streamline operations.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the landscape of Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) advertising by creating immersive, high-impact experiences that captivate audiences where they are most engaged. We are committed to breaking down barriers, making powerful advertising accessible to all businesses, from innovative startups to established brands, and driving unparalleled brand visibility and interaction.

Our Vision

To redefine the future of advertising by seamlessly integrating brands into the daily lives of their target audience. We envision a world where every brand can effortlessly connect with people in moments that matter, delivering messages that resonate and inspire action—transforming public spaces into dynamic, storytelling platforms.
